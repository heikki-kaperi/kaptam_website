Lahti's Board Games Hub

Weekly starting 6.2.2026

Board games evening is hosted Mondays 16:00 - 20:30 at Niemenkatu 73, near the lobby at Kanttis restaurant area. Free and open for everybody.

Over 45 unique games, including multiple copies of popular games.





Guide for arriving to weekly session






Lahti Science Park
Niemenkatu 73
15140 Lahti
Lobby near the front door reception
Google Maps
Enter from the main entrance (revolving door) or from D1. If you arrive before 16:00, you can just walk in. If you arrive after 16:00, you'll need to ask somebody from our WhatsApp & Discord channels, or phone number +358 40 850 5051 to open the door for you. Links for joining them are in to bottom of this webpage.
