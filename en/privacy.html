Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Kaptam Gamers ry ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our game reservation system on kaptam.fi.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you make a game reservation through our website, we collect:

  • Your username
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Controller preference (mouse & keyboard or game controller)
  • Visit date (optional)
  • Additional information you choose to provide (optional)
  • List of games you've selected for reservation

Automatically Stored Information

Our website uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to:

  • Maintain your game selection cart while browsing
  • Remember that you've dismissed the welcome message
  • Store your reservation code for reference

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and managing your game reservations
  • Installing requested games before your visit
  • Contacting you if necessary regarding your reservation
  • Providing you with your reservation code

We do not:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Use your information for marketing purposes
  • Share your information with anyone outside our organization
  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Use analytics or advertising cookies

Data Storage

  • Reservation information is stored on our server only for the purpose of fulfilling your reservation
  • Your browser's localStorage contains temporary data (cart contents, preferences) that remains on your device
  • You can clear localStorage data at any time through your browser settings

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

Data Retention

We retain your reservation information only as long as necessary to fulfill the reservation and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping purposes. After this period, your data is deleted.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
