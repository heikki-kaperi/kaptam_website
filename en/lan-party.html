Lahti Lan Party

8-10.5.2026
Computer 30 spots 5 €
Visitor 20 tickets 2 €

We've organized a LAN party with the help of Lahden Verkkopalvelu Oy to have 3 days, 2 nights of gaming fun with like-minded people (technology & gaming enthusiasts).

Niemenkatu 73 (Lahti Science Park) Pöllö-auditorium, 15140 Lahti Google maps
Anybody over 16 years old can join in

A LAN party is a social gathering of participants with computers or compatible game consoles, where a local area network (LAN) connection, primarily for the purpose of playing multiplayer games together. They generally require participants to bring their own PC.
