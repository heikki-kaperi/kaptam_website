Sundays 14:00 - 17:30
Puolenkuun Pelit

Lahti FGC (fighting game community) gaming evenings contain 5 PC gaming setups, controllers and over 20 different games that anybody over 12 can play (most of our games are PEGI 12 age rated). PC setups are offline only.

If you want, you can bring your own controller & system.



The fighting game genre of video games involves combat between characters often in one-on-one battles. They often features mechanics such as blocking, grappling, counter-attacking, and chaining attacks together into "combos". Characters engage in hand-to-hand combat, while some games may include weaponry.
