About us

Kaptam Gamers started 2024 and became an association in 2025. We are enthusiastic gamers with experience in video production for companies, PC building, playing various board games and years of experience in video gaming.

In 2025, in addition to weekly club game nights, we held public LAN party and three fighting game tournaments. In the future, we will continue weekly activities and monthly tournaments, while improving our operations to be better for our visitors.

Anyone can attend our game nights (except for board game nights only for LAB/LUT students and staff). Instructions for attending are found their specific club pages, links in the top bar.

We publish the latest news in Whatsapp groups and Discord, links in the bottom of this page. You can see the newest pictures of our activities on Instagram.
