Sivusto suomeksi

Kaptam gamers is an association for nourishing video gaming, board gaming, role-playing hobbies in the Päijät-Häme area. Our mission is to make onsite activities for these hobbies easily accessible, so that everybody can try and enjoy these hobbies together.

“We are a democracy. We are doing it for the people”.
