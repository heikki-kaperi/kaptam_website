Lahti Fisticuffs

Fighting game tournament

Open tournament for new and experienced fighting game players to have fun. We have many PC setups, where you can play with and controller. You don't need to bring anything (other than phone. You'll need that to get inside the event area). Event is also streamed in twitch, in the channel: Juusodotexe
Free for anybody over 12 years old to join in.

Winner prize:
1st 20 €
2nd   10 €
3rd 5 €

~ Tournament game ~
BlazBlue Centralfiction ~ Rules ~
The tournament is run on the double elimination format

Matches are best of 3. Top 4 matches are best of 5 (Winners Finals, Losers Semis, Losers Finals, Grand Finals)

The loser of a match can change their character, meanwhile the winner has to stay on their chosen character

Built in Macro buttons in the collection for supers and specials are banned

Tournament controllers (xbox 360 style controllers & PS4 controllers) are available for players, but you are welcome to bring your own controller. The only exception is a controller with built-in macro buttons
