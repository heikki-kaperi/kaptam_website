Mounthly tournament LAHTI FISTICUFFS

Lahti Fisticuffs is a monthly open tournament for new and experienced fighting game players to have fun.

Free for anybody over 13 years old to join in.

Currently, we don't have an exact date, on when the next one is held. We will inform here and in our other channels, when we do.







The fighting game genre of video games involves combat between characters often in one-on-one battles. They often features mechanics such as blocking, grappling, counter-attacking, and chaining attacks together into "combos". Characters engage in hand-to-hand combat, while some games may include weaponry.
