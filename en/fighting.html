Lahti Fisticuffs

Fighting game tournament

Open tournament for new and experienced fighting game players to have fun. We have many PC setups, where you can play with and controller. You don't need to bring anything (other than phone. You'll need that to get inside the event area). Event is also streamed in twitch, in the channel: Juusodotexe
Free for anybody over 15 years old to join in.

Winner prize:
1st 20 €
2nd   10 €
3rd 5 €


Guide for arriving to the event

Lahti Science Park
Niemenkatu 73
15140 Lahti

Enter from the main entrance (revolving door) or from D1. If you arrive before 16:00, you can just walk in. If you arrive after 16:00, you'll need to ask somebody from our WhatsApp / Discord channels, or text +358408505051 to open the door for you. Links for joining them are in to bottom of this webpage.

~ Tournament game ~
Vampire Savior ~ Rules ~
The tournament format is double elimination. If you lose once, you are not out of the tournament yet, but lose again and you are out of the tournament

Tournament will be played on Capcom Fighting Collection using the Japanese version on turbo 3

The matches are ft2. This means that the first player to win 2 games out of 3 wins the set

Oboro Bishamon character is banned

After one match, the losing player can change characters. The winning player must play with the same character as in the last match

Fetus of God stage is banned due to being a different length than the other stages

Built in Macro buttons in the collection for supers and specials are banned

Tournament controllers (xbox 360 style controllers, couple of PS4 controllers) are available for players, but you are welcome to bring your own controller. The only exception is a controller with built-in macro buttons

The winner of the tournament will face a strong opponent. If you lose, you will still get the prize money, but you will lose the bragging rights ~ Last tournament bracket ~
